# 🌍 EcoTrace Ultimate ♻️

### 🚀 Multi-Impact Environmental Tracking & Sustainability Intelligence Platform

**EcoTrace Ultimate** is a premium, high-fidelity **Single Page Application (SPA)** designed to help individuals **measure, understand, monitor, and reduce** their environmental impact in real time.

By combining advanced ecological calculations with gamification and AI-driven sustainability guidance, EcoTrace Ultimate empowers users to build greener habits and move toward a **low-carbon, climate-conscious lifestyle**.

---

## 🌱 What EcoTrace Measures

EcoTrace goes beyond traditional carbon calculators by tracking multiple environmental indicators:

🌫️ **Carbon Emissions (CO₂)**
💧 **Virtual Water Footprint**
🌎 **Ecological Land Demand (Global Hectares - gha)**
🛍️ **Plastic Waste Generation**
⚡ **Energy Consumption Impact**
♻️ **Waste & Recycling Performance**

All calculations update dynamically as users interact with the platform.
